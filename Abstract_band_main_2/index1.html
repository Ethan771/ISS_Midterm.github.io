Introduction to Abstract

Hello! We're Abstract band, hailing from Dukekunshan University. Our name stems from a cyber word commonly used in China to express bewilderment or confusion. While some might not always understand us, we embrace the abstract, and believe it mirrors our outlook on life. Over the years, we've explored genres like pop, ACG, rock, and R&B, but our musical journey doesn't stop there. We're always eager to experiment and grow.
